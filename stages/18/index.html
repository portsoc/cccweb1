A New Platform

After thirty years in development The Web has evolved to become a stack of independlently evolving technologies driven by hundreds of thousands of developers from and billions of users worldwide.

The web permeates all societies. It powers, intergrates with, or supports almost every computer system in use today. It is arguably the most important platform in computing history, and the most important invention since the wheel.

Our aim is to pass on the knowledge, skills and inspiration necessary for you to take maximum advantage of this world-altering potential.
